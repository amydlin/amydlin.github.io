Product Design Internship at NVIDIA

Internship

Aug 2018 — Dec 2018

Supervised by

Karen Dryden↗︎

I worked on GeForce NOW, a new cloud gaming platform in its beta stage. GeForce NOW bridges NVIDIA's elite hardware performance with an extensive gaming library. I was the first intern in the history of the team staffed to a consumer-facing product.

A few highlights and accomplishments

Redesigned the settings page entirely on my own.

Collaborated with visual designers in the redesign of the card component, which informs everything else (similar to the importance of a content card on Netflix).

Led user research to understand user mental models.

Due to an understaffed research team and everyone else being uncomfortable with speaking to external users, I wrote the research protocol and led a team of five designers (many being seniors!) in interviews that helped inform our roadmap and architecture.

Set up an affinity diagram and led my team through the process of grouping learnings.

I focused the activity around user goals since it was something I felt had been lacking in conversations.

Assisted in retooling the product's architecture to fit users' mental models.

What it looked like before

Early research insights clearly showed that the MVP of this product was clunky and dramatically different from users' understanding. I was an active member of the team and collaborated with the development and marketing teams to work towards a more usable experience.

After, with a focus on users "snacking" to browse.

Helped organize and participated in an intra-team workshop to blend ideas.

Attendees included engineering and business team members at all levels to educate about design process and prioritize project goals.

Bonus~

NVIDIA PR did a photoshoot with me, my team, and my affinity diagram that they still use in promotional materials.
